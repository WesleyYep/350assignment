These sans serifs are constructed of straight, monolinear lines and circular or square shapes. 
Mimics the circular pitch and straight goal posts of quidditch, but also quite simple.
The starkness of most geometric sans serifs makes for great headings, but they are usually less than ideal for long paragraphs.
