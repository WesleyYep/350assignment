Welcome to SQRL

Background

Staying around for the summer? Come along and join the summer Quidditch recreational league for 2015. Guaranteed to be a great way to have fun, keep fit, and play with friends and family.


About

SQRL is a league for players of all ages and abilities.

  • Feel like playing with work mates?
  • Feel like playing more competitively?
  • Are your kids budding Quidditch superstars?
  • Sign up and join in the fun!

Location

We play in the following locations:

  • Greater London
  • Scotland
  • Northern Ireland
  • Wales
  • Midlands
  • Northern England
  • Eastern England
  • Southern England
